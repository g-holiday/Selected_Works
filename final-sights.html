Sights

The scenery in Scotland is quite varied; from ancient ruins and castles to shimmering cityscapes and bucolic countryside, Scotland has something that will pique anyone's curiosity and also appeal to one's inner aesthetician.

Left, Top: Eilean Donan Castle, Loch Duich, western Highlands. Left, Center: the Clyde Arc road bridge, Glasgow. Left, Bottom: the ruins of the Cathedral of Saint Andrew, St Andrews, Fife. Farther Above: view of Balephuil Bay, Tiree, Inner Hebrides. Directly Above: monument built in honor of William Wallace who was lionized in the film Braveheart, Abbey Craig, Stirling.
