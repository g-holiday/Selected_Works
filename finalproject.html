Scotland

Scotland is a constituent country of the UK which occupies the northern third of the island of Britain situated in northwestern Europe.The city of Edinburgh is its capital, and just over 5.2 million people reside in the country. Instead of paying the silly, inflated prices of London and Southern England, come discover a lesser-known side of Britain.
